# Product Vision and Business Model — Gigio Flow

> This file is the source of truth about the business purpose. The PM Agent and CEO Agent use it to ensure new features are aligned with the company strategy.

---

## 🎯 1. Mission Statement and Purpose

-   **The Mission:** Transform any Markdown workspace into an Agile Development Operating System, where AI teams and humans collaborate in a structured, traceable, and scalable way to deliver digital products with speed and quality.
-   **The Customer Pain to Cure:** Solopreneurs and small development teams lose context between AI sessions, have no governance over what the AI does, and spend more time managing the AI than delivering product. The result is chaotic deploys, untracked technical debt, and always-outdated documentation.
-   **The Proposed Solution:** A standardized Markdown file structure + a local visual panel (Studio) that serves as the project's "nervous system" — keeping context, rules, AI personas, and the history of decisions always synchronized and readable by any state-of-the-art AI agent (Claude, Gemini, GPT-4, Cursor, Windsurf).

---

## 📊 2. Business Model and Monetization

-   **Primary Target Audience:** Technical solopreneurs, indie founders, freelance developers, and small product squads (2-5 people) who use AI as the primary development lever.
-   **Monetization Strategy:** MIT Open-Source model with monetization via: (1) Paid Premium Templates with pre-configured stacks per vertical (SaaS, e-commerce, mobile app); (2) Gigio Flow Cloud — hosted version with multi-device sync and real-time collaboration; (3) Assisted Setup Service for companies that want to implement Gigio Flow in larger teams.
-   **Acquisition Channels:** Organic distribution via GitHub (open-source), content marketing on Twitter/X and YouTube showcasing real AI workflows, developer communities (Dev.to, Hacker News), and partnerships with AI productivity content creators.

---

## 🛡️ 3. Non-Negotiable Product Values

1.  **Permanent Context:** The AI should never "forget" the project. The Markdown files are the persistent memory that survives any session reset.
2.  **Human in Control:** The AI executes and proposes, the human approves and decides. No destructive action is irreversible without explicit approval.
3.  **Total Portability:** The workspace works in any editor, with any AI, on any operating system. There is no platform lock-in.
4.  **Radical Transparency:** Every AI decision is logged in `HISTORICO.md`. The process is auditable by humans and other AIs.
