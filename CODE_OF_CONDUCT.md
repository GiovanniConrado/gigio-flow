# Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

We want Gigio Flow to be a practical, respectful, and useful open-source project for maintainers, builders, and AI tooling contributors.

Participants are expected to communicate with patience, clarity, and respect.

## Expected Behavior

- Be kind and direct.
- Assume good intent, but clarify when needed.
- Keep feedback specific and actionable.
- Respect different experience levels.
- Focus disagreement on ideas, code, documentation, and process.

## Unacceptable Behavior

- harassment, threats, or personal attacks
- discriminatory language or behavior
- publishing private information without permission
- repeated disruption of project discussions
- intentionally submitting malicious code or secret-stealing behavior

## Enforcement

Maintainers may remove comments, close issues, reject contributions, or block participants who violate this code of conduct.
